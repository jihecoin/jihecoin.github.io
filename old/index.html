Jagriti Institute of Higher Education is run by Jagriti Shiksha Samiti Mohna. The college is situated in Mohna, Ballabgarh, Distt-Faridabad Haryana. We are running a course for Bachelor of Education and Diploma in Education. Our aim is to provide opportunities for all students to achieve their full potential and this can be done through collective efforts of faculty, staff members and students. The college provides all those facilities which are essential for making would be teachers.

The course Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) is meant for preparing pillars of nation.  The duration of course for Bachelor of Education is one year and the minimum eligibility is graduation with 50%* or above marks (prescribed by the state) and the course Diploma in Education (D.Ed.)

 
 
 

Vision

  
 

We are eager to impart a sound formation in all fields such as social, moral, economic, academic, physical, emotional through 'Teach One – Each One'.

 
 

Mission

  
 

Our society is eager to in thrust the skills with competencies, intellectual leadership, scholarly contributions, design of courses, experimental learning, solving educational problems among the pupil-teachers to prepare them to face challenges in a rapidly changing world.

 
 

Value

  
 

All our activities and programmes are to increase social, economic, religious & physical equality. Special focus has been given to protect, conservate the nature and environment through our institution.
