ABOUT MANAGEMENT

Jagriti institute of higher education has been working with the idea better education for better society. This aim can be achieved through a quality education and now our endeavor is to create such educator who can impart quality education.

At Jagriti Institute of Higher education is to set learning challenges in carrying and orderly environment where pupil-teacher`s are provided opportunities for social emotional, cultural, mental and physical development. Jagriti Institute of higher education offers a broad teacher education where academic excellence is complimented by cultural, sporting and social development. We strive to develop each pupil teacher`s potentials, recognize his or her dignity and encourage positive self-esteem in order to promote responsibility and creativity dedicated nation and society.

I wish you all the Success.
